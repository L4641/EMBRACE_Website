Grandparents Learning English

To help Asian seniors overcome language barriers, EMBRACE launched a youth-led YouTube channel called "Grandparents Learning English". We invite youths interested in helping Asian seniors to join us!
Why: Language is the primary barrier faced by senior immigrants. We need YOUR help to support them with English.
Who: Anyone in grades K-12 interested in helping senior immigrants to learn English.
What: Make a short video on a chosen topic to teach English words/ phrases. Video Requirements:

  • Maximum 5 minutes
  • Landscape view (horizontal)
  • Include 5-10 English words in a chosen topic

Contact us for video submission or any other questions.
