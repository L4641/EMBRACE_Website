Mission

"We hope to improve the well-being of our senior Asian community within the Greater Cincinnati area by providing a comforting community and informative resources."

How it started

Founded from a research project conducted by EMBRACE President Vivian Xu, her interests led to the question of: "What factors are associated with the well-being of older Chinese immigrants in Cincinnati?"

Below are some of the steps taken from the research project:

01
Preliminary Research

First, it was noted how the senior Asian population in Cincinnati was growing significantly over the years. However, this was peculiar as Cincinnati has a much smaller Asian population when compared to other major cities. This brought up the possible concern of the well-being of this group.

02
The Current Study

Now that the focus group has been identified, some goals were created in order to fully explore their overall satisfaction living in the Cincinnati region.

Research Goals

  • Create a baseline for older Chinese immigrants’ demographics in Cincinnati
  • Quantify their well-being and find associated factors
  • Understand Pandemic’s impact to their lives
  • Identify preferred activities to improve life satisfaction
  • What factors are associated with the well-being of older Chinese immigrants in Cincinnati?
